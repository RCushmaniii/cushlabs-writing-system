Voice DNA Creator

Structured interview prompt that extracts tone, rhythm, and distinctive language patterns to generate voice-dna.json.

Purpose

This prompt guides a conversation designed to capture how the business sounds — the distinctive patterns that make content recognizable as "yours," not generic AI output.

Expected Output

A complete voice-dna.json file containing:

  • Core tone and attitude
  • Sentence rhythm and structure preferences
  • Distinctive phrases and words to favor
  • Explicit boundaries (what never to say)
  • Punctuation and formatting style
