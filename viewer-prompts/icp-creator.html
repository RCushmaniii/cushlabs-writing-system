ICP Creator

Structured interview prompt that captures audience intelligence to generate icp.json.

Purpose

This prompt guides a conversation designed to extract deep audience understanding — the pains, language, objections, and cultural context that prevent generic marketing speak.

Expected Output

A complete icp.json file containing:

  • Primary audience definition and geographic segments
  • Core pains and desired outcomes
  • Language the audience actually uses (often bilingual)
  • Jargon to avoid and plain language alternatives
  • Objections and validated rebuttals
  • Buying triggers and ideal customer signals
