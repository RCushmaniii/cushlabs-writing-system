Business Profile Creator

Structured interview prompt that documents offers, pricing, and positioning to generate business-profile.json.

Purpose

This prompt simulates a thorough professional interview with a business owner to extract factual information about what the business offers, how it's positioned, and what can be claimed.

Expected Output

A complete business-profile.json file containing:

  • Business name, tagline, and positioning statement
  • Brand voice rules (first-person vs. we, tone)
  • Service offerings with deliverables and pricing
  • Proof points and credibility mechanisms
  • Competitive differentiation
  • CTAs and booking mechanisms
