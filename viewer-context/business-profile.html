Business Profile

Defines what the business offers — services, pricing, positioning, proof points, and CTAs. Prevents invented offers.

About This File

When to load: When CTAs, offers, pricing, deliverables, or positioning are present

The Business Profile acts as a single source of truth for business facts. It includes:

  • Business name, tagline, and positioning statement
  • Brand voice rules (first-person vs. we, tone, enforcement)
  • Service offerings with deliverables and pricing
  • Proof points and credibility mechanisms
  • Competitive differentiation
  • CTAs and booking mechanisms
